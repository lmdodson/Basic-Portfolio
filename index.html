About Me

My name is Leah Dodson, and I'm working hard to learn full-stack web development. I have spent the last decade as a writer, and I love language and storytelling. I hope to combine the two interests and use web development as another medium for telling stories and connecting with others.


When I'm not stashed away behind the computer, I love to be active and adventurous. I love rock climbing, hiking, riding my motorcycle, and hanging out with my husband and daughter. I'm passionate about continual learning and bewing open to new experiences.
